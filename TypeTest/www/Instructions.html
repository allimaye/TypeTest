Instructions
  1. 1. Fill out user information
  2. 2. After clicking "OK", you will see a signal to indicate you should start typing
  3. 3. Type the paragraph in colored words into the text box above it as fast as you can.
  4. 4. Look in the statistics tab to see your typing speed and accuracy

    5. A few extra notes:

    • Click sound icon (show icon here) to turn on dictation. The next word you have to type will be read to you.
    • You will notice that the words are color coded to show which words you have completed, which word you are currently on, and which words you still need to type.
